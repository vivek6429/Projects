Chimei Innolux N156B6-L0B Overview

The N156B6-L0B (Product Code: LEN40B0) is a 15.6 inch diagonal a-Si TFT-LCD display panel product from Chimei Innolux Corporation (hereafter called Chimei Innolux), with an integral WLED backlight system, With LED Driver , without touch screen. It features an operating temperature range of 0 ~ 50°C , a storage temperature range of -20 ~ 60°C . It's general features are summarized by Panelook in the following: Glare Surface, Landscape type, White LED Backlight, Wide Aspect Ratio, Signal Interface Position Reverse . Based on its features, Panelook recommend that this model be applied to Laptop etc. According to the information stored in Panelook this model mass production on Q4, 2009, Now this model is discontinued. There are 9 items stock and 7 suppliers of this model on Panelook. We inputted this model's specification on May 16 2012 for the first time, and the latest update on Mar 1 2018. If you want to embed N156B6-L0B LCM in your future product, Panelook strongly recommend that you should contact with Chimei Innolux or it's agent to learn lateast production and specification detail.
